## Technical Aspects to evaluate

**Naming.** Are variables/functions/methods correctly named? do the express what they contain/do?

**Documentation.** Is there any comment to explain the solution or to ease the understanding of the code?

**Magic numbers.** Are numbers defined by constant or any other way to express what are they for?

**Correctness.** Does the solution have the requested behavior? Are corner-cases correctly handled?

**Performance.** Even though the performance is not always evaluated, does the solution consider possible memory/time performance issues?

**Modularization.** Is the solution implemented in one 
single big method? Are there some functions/methods that modularize the 
code in order to separate responsibilities and improve readability?

**OOP.** Are involved entities correctly represented as classes? Do they have the correct responsibility?

**Scalability.** Is it consider any way of scaling the solution? For example adding new types of input,

**Error handling.** Are errors properly handled? Are they catched in the correct place according to each class/method/function responsibility?
